Our Actual Plan

As you can tell, we are fans of the Batman Trilogy. In all seriousness, here is our actual plan:

There were two life-long friends, a computer programmer and a mechanical engineer/robotics expert. Alone, they traversed their individual professions, learning the industry and finding their own ideas. Then, they decided to start a company to combine their skills. Before anyone knew it, they were up and running, bringing the newest edge to the future. It was named Thunder, in honor of the mystical beast, the Thunder Horse, who dwells in the Astral Planes, only descending to earth to rally us with its mighty thunder. The Great Lightning Wolf battles with the Thunder Horse, as a matter of majestic honor and tradition. In truth, they greet as old friends, and are bound by mutual respect.
